Features

Everything a birder needs

From backyard sparrows to exotic jungle birds — AvianCall has every call, chirp, and song you're looking for.

🎙️

Pro-quality recordings

Every sound recorded in the field by professional ornithologists. No synthesized audio — 100% real.

📍

Region filtering

Browse birds by continent, country, or biome. Find exactly what you'd hear on your next hike.

🔍

Smart search

Search by common name, scientific name, or habitat. Instant results as you type.

📥

Offline playback

Download your favorites and listen anywhere — no signal needed in the field.

❤️

Personal collection

Save your favorite birds and build your own personal sightings list with notes.

🌿

New birds weekly

Our team of ornithologists adds fresh recordings every week. The library never stops growing.
